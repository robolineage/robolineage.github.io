RoboLineage

Agent-native data lifecycle governance across robot policy iterations. RoboLineage turns rollouts, reviews, datasets, training runs, evaluations, and next-collection decisions into linked lifecycle artifacts.

Qian Luo, Wentao Guo, Zhennan Qin, Nanchun Guo, Yunhan Zhao, Yi Ma, Yanchao Yang

Paper Code Videos

System Overview

Making policy iteration legible across data, training, evaluation, and recollection.

RoboLineage treats robot policy iteration as a lifecycle of typed artifacts. Agents interpret embodied evidence and prepare structured outputs; schemas, validators, and operator decisions keep lifecycle state inspectable across iterations.

RoboLineage follows the full robot policy iteration loop, from robot onboarding and semantic capture to dataset admission, training integration, evaluation, and next-collection planning.

Review Pipeline

Raw capture, online semantic anchors, and asynchronous post-rollout review run as separate lanes.

Raw robot recording remains the source of truth. The online Visual Snapshot Agent writes sparse task-aware anchors during collection, while post-rollout review aggregates evidence packets after raw capture closes.

The three-lane design preserves raw recording, gives operators timely feedback, and leaves stricter dataset admission to asynchronous review.

Qualitative Results

Visual evidence becomes governed lifecycle state.

VSA and post-rollout review convert sparse visual moments into evidence packets, review outcomes, dataset decisions, and next-collection hints.

Closed-Loop Recollection

Failure evidence becomes data-collection action.

RoboLineage links review evidence, dataset state, evaluation outcomes, and previous policy iterations so that next-collection plans target the failure mode currently blocking improvement.

Code and Release Plan
